Design details

Architecture

  • PFH: Progressive Flex Hinge with cam + dual-rate elastomers (boot-flex ~90→130).
  • TVSM: Torsion + Vertical Shock Module (±25–30° elastic twist; 15–20 mm travel).
  • BIP: DIN-compatible binding interface with optional fuse screws.

Materials

7075-T6/Ti-6Al-4V links, 17-4PH pins, PTFE-lined bearings, low-temp oil & seals, CF foot element.

Validation

FEA sizing to ~800 N·m bending; cold-soak; endurance cycles; on-hill adjustability with gloved controls.
